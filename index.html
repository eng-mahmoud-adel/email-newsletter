Summer + Ignition Sources = ?...

Remember:

  • High temperature can act as an ignition source
  • Regulate environmental and hot surface temperatures
  • Monitor ignition sources and controls in high temperatures
  • Fire can start and spread quickly in hot weather

Be extra FIRE AWARE in hot weather
